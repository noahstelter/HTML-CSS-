Moon Knight

Moon Knight is riddled with chaos

Moon Knight is a character from Marvel comics based around Egyptian mythology. The moon god Konshu seeks out vengeance on people who wish to do harm unto other humans. Just one problem is he cannot act alone, he needs an avatar to help serve him in justice. Enter Marc Spector a man unsure of his reality. A man with split personalities and creating alter egos. Steven Grant a well mannered man who often is an escape for Marc's horrors. Jake Lockely is a man of many mysteries. Most unknown to the either two. Jake is very present throughout the show but hides quite a bit. What more can Marc and Steven discover throughout the show?
